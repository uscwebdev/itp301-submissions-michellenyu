Website Topic

Testing a creative approach to designing a ux design portfolio. I want to display my personality while making the design process very clear.

Audience

My intended audience are recruiters for entry level ux design work.

Design and Color Scheme

The design is more free flowing in the main page, and then more structured in the project pages. The color scheme will be mainly white, black, and gray, and with emphasis with other pops of colors, such as beige, red, blue, and purple.
